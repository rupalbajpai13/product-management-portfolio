# Learning Portfolio

## Overview

This section documents my continuous learning journey in Product Management, Agile practices, and Artificial Intelligence.

Rather than simply completing online courses, I apply the concepts to real-world product scenarios and document my understanding through case studies, reflections, and practical product artifacts.

The objective is to continuously strengthen my Product Management skills while building products using modern AI technologies.

---

## Learning Areas

### IBM Product Management

A collection of exercises, case studies, and reflections completed as part of the IBM Product Management program. Each module includes practical application of Product Management concepts to real product scenarios.

### SAFe® Product Owner/Product Manager (POPM)

Notes, frameworks, and practical learnings from the SAFe POPM certification, including Product Ownership, PI Planning, Backlog Management, WSJF prioritization, and Agile Product Delivery.

### AI Product Management

Exploration of Generative AI, AI Agents, Prompt Engineering, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), MCP, and Agentic AI from a Product Manager's perspective.

---

## Learning Approach

For every topic, I follow a structured approach:

- Learn the concept
- Complete the practical exercise
- Reflect on the key learnings
- Apply the concept to one of my product ideas
- Document the outcome

This portfolio demonstrates my commitment to continuous learning and practical application of Product Management principles.
